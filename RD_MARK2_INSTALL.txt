What this version does vs RD Mark 1:
- Reuses the Mark 1 model (same silhouette, new texture colour).
- Textures shifted to a slightly cooler steel-grey tone (less rusty, more refined).
- Slightly better fall resistance (10 → 12).
- Very slightly improved speed and jump (one small step in the mk1→mk5 chain).
- Flamethrower fuel burns marginally more efficiently (drain 0.023 vs 0.025).
- Still no flight — that comes in a later RD mark.
- Still no arm rocket — that comes in a later RD mark.
- Fabricator requires RD Mark 1 to be built first (sind:rd_mark1).
