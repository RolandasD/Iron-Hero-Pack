What each version does vs its predecessor:

RD Mark 3 / Plated Chassis (vs Mark 2):
- Textures shifted to cool grey-steel (hue rotation +10deg, saturation x0.60).
- Better fall resistance (12 → 13).
- Slightly improved speed and jump.
- Flamethrower fuel burns more efficiently (drain 0.021 vs 0.023).
- Still no flight.
- Fabricator requires RD Mark 2 (sind:rd_mark2).

RD Mark 4 / Proto Booster (vs Mark 3):
- Textures shifted to darker charcoal with a blue-grey tinge (hue +20deg, saturation x0.40).
- PUNCH_DAMAGE increases to 4 (first punch upgrade in the chain).
- Better fall resistance (13 → 15).
- Noticeably improved speed and jump.
- Flamethrower fuel burns more efficiently (drain 0.019).
- Still no flight.
- Fabricator requires RD Mark 3 (sind:rd_mark3).

RD Mark 5 / Pre-Ignition (vs Mark 4):
- Textures shifted to near-silver / polished steel (hue +25deg, saturation x0.22).
- Tier bumps to 5.
- Better fall resistance (15 → 17).
- Improved speed and jump, approaching final Mark 1 values.
- Flamethrower still available, fuel drain 0.017.
- FLIGHT ENABLED — first suit in the RD prototype chain with propelled flight.
  (propelled_flight is no longer blocked in isModifierEnabled.)
- Fabricator requires RD Mark 4 (sind:rd_mark4).
